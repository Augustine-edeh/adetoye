About Me

My name is Adetoye, and I'm a front-end web developer. I specialize in front-end website development utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm also well-versed with PHP and the React JS framework.

I design websites for a variety of purposes, including landing pages, e-commerce storefronts, blogs, portfolios, and commercial websites, and I optimize them for the best possible user experience.

Problem solving is my favorite aspect of my job. I enjoy working with businesses and assisting them in determining what type of website is ideal for their needs.

Before switching to a professional career, I started programming as a hobby, creating a variety of fun projects, this makes it very easy for me to solve challenging tasks because I always find an interesting way to break it down into smaller tasks while having fun.

I'm also interested in sales and marketing, and I enjoy advising businesses and startups on how to best increase revenue through their websites. My goal is to assist as many businesses as possible in getting the most out of their websites in terms of generating traffic, increasing revenue, and providing the best user experiences.
